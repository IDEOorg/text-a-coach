Why are we doing this?

Text-a-coach is brought to you by the financial health tools team at IDEO.org, a group of creative problem-solvers that is tackling the world’s biggest challenges. We’ve created this tool as a part of one of our design programs, and our goal is to learn how people might benefit from a service like this. We are grateful if you’d be willing to try it out!
